Red Velvet Cupcakes

These red velvet cupcakes are mini versions of classic Red Velvet Cake, a popular offering at bakeries all across the country. Whip up a batch this holiday season or any time of the year.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Milk
  • Red food coloring
  • Vanilla extract

For the frosting:

  • Cream cheese
  • Butter
  • Sour cream
  • Vanilla
  • Confector's sugar

Steps

  1. Make the batter
  2. Bake the cupcakes
  3. Frost the cupcakes

You can find the original recipe at allrecipes.com
